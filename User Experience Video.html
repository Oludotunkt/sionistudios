RTV Virtual Tour Solution

Offers 360 degree virtual tour and tracks engagement from prospective cusotmers.


The RTV virtual tour platform, comes with a fully responsive HTML 5 virtual tour technology, providing business owners and in-house data analysists the information needed to take informed decisions. This is apt for the real estate or data-driven products/projects. Take your time and explore the many features & benefits that are available your business offerings / projects.

Features & Benefits

Yearly Hosting | Perodic Traffic Report | Floor Plan.

Perodic Traffic Report

The periodic virtual tour traffic reporting now feature a full breakdown of traffic and data by day, month, quarter, and year. You’ll see the number of times your virtual tour has been emailed, shared on social sites, and viewed on a mobile phone. All reports are automatically sent to your customers from YOUR email address.

QR Code

You can automatically rip any of your virtual tours to a QR code. You can also have a printable size of the QR code. Best of all, the RTV platform will show your clients how many visitors have scanned the QR code each week on mobile devices. 

Embedding Codes + Links 

The RTV platform provides Javascript for easy embedding of published tour. Among the deliverables are transformation of long virtual tour link into smaller, more manageable URL with TinyTURLS (Tiny Tour URLs). This solution automatically generates a custom, short URL that points directly to your online virtual tour.   

Customisable Features 

Lead Grabber is great option to turn on that collects data from virtual tour viewers. You can also integrate floor plans into your virttual tour, which is ideal for the real estate industry. 

Periodic Traffic Report

It shows the traffic data and the different online sources as well as the level of engagement per scene.

Yearly Hosting subscription

The RTV platform has a yearly hosting fee which helps to maintain the hosted virtual tour  and its engagement over the period of subscription.

Scalable

One of the unique features of this solution is the ability to make changes, like reviewing scenes as well as add or delete scenes to reflect on-site realities. These changes can be made at anytime after publishing the tour.

Works with /withour a Website.

One of advantages of the virtual tour is that it can operate with or without a website. Whether yours is a short termed project for real estate or a long termed project like a school, hotel, etc. It doesn't need a website to run, as it is hosted like other websites.

PC / Mobile Compartible

Knowing that over 70% of smartphones users use their phones for location-based solution, the RTV virtual tour solution is compartible with PCs and mobile devices alike. This allows online visitors and prospective customers view your business offerings/projects via their mobile devices..
